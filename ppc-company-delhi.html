Frequently Asked Questions?

1What is PPC advertising?

PPC (Pay-Per-Click) advertising is a digital marketing strategy where advertisers pay a fee each time their ad is clicked. It's a way of buying visits to your site rather than attempting to earn those visits organically.

2What are the benefits of PPC?
  • Immediate results: PPC campaigns can drive immediate traffic and results to your website.
  • Targeted advertising: PPC allows you to target your ads to specific audiences based on demographics, interests, and behavior.
  • Cost-effective: You only pay when someone clicks on your ad, making it a cost-effective advertising method.
  • Measurable results: PPC campaigns provide detailed analytics and metrics to track the performance of your ads.
